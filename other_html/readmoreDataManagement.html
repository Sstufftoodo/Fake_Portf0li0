Although i don't use other DBMS
I sometimes dabble in MariaDB

MariaDB is more scalable and offers a higher query speed when compared to MySQL. This makes it good for managing large-sized data. You will also find more features in MariaDB that MySQL doesn't have, like sequence storage engines and virtual columns.
